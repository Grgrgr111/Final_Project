Milan Fashion Week

Established in 1958, MFW is part of the global "Big Four fashion weeks". The autumn/winter event is held in February/March of each year, and the spring/summer event is held in September/October of each year. These is a collection of the models-off-duty snapped by me in 2018 and 2019.
